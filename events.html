Group 1

Ignite

  • POSTER
  • RULES

“IGNITE” is an event based on startup ideas. The idea must be related to Civil Engineering.

  • Team comprises Maximum of 4 members of same college.
  • Event consists of 3 Rounds – Submission of abstracts, Presentation of the startup plan, test on innovative strategies.
  • Prototype of product/process in any form will be appreciated.

Group 2

Utopian

  • POSTER
  • RULES

A complete package of technical skills and knowledge are to be applied to become a Mr. / Miss. Utopian.

Rotolare

  • POSTER
  • RULES

Rotolare event is a ride of marbles on a track. The marble is of glass and the track consists of sudden jumps, drops, loops, turns, and obstacles. The only driving force is of gravitational pull. The time used by marble to ride from starting point to end point and the complexity of the track will be considered for judging.

  • The event is open to students of all branches
  • Maximum participants in each team are 3
  • Two teams should not have any common member
  • There will be two rounds,
    • 1. Screening round (Based on basics of Physics and Engineering Mechanics)
    2. Final round (Rotolare making)
  • Top 20 teams will be selected for the Final Round
  • Details of Screening Round and Final Round will be announced on the spot
Student Coordinators:
  • Sushmita Loni -9448805329
  • Manjunath Nagur -9535930203

Group 3

Minute to win it

  • POSTER
  • RULES

The participants are expected to visually identify and guess materials and objects related to the field of study of Civil Engineering. This is to be done in a period of one minute only, as the name suggests. The fastest team wins the prize.

  • Team size: 2 members
  • The event will consist of two rounds.
  • Round 1: Screening.
  • Round 2: Finals.
  • 10 teams will qualify to the finals.
  • The detailed rules of the event will be disclosed on spot.
